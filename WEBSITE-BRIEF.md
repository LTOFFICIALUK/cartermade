# CarterMade — full website brief

## Goal

A portfolio site so composed and confident that a small-business owner concludes: *this is the developer I want*. Proof is the live work. Craft is the brand. WhatsApp is the door.

## Positioning

**CarterMade** builds websites for small local businesses — UK-wide. Not an agency deck. Not a template mill. A one-person studio where the site itself is the pitch.

## Brand: atelier, not café

“Made” suggests handmade. Lean into **workshop / atelier**: walnut grain as texture (subtle), warm ivory paper, brass accents, Fraunces for the wordmark — like a craftsman’s stamp on modern work.

**Avoid:** barn wood, latte foam, heavy distressed textures, purple SaaS, cream+terracotta cliché, newspaper broadsheet.

## Audience

Owners of local service businesses (garages, accountants, gyms, trades, creators) who need a site that looks serious and converts — phone calls, bookings, enquiries.

## Motion principles

- Sections lock in like puzzle pieces (opacity + slight Y, staggered children)
- Work cards: soft reveal on scroll, optional gentle parallax on media
- No bounce, no random floating badges, no hero overlays
- Honour `prefers-reduced-motion`
