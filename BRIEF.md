## Brand

- **Direction:** Atelier / workshop — “handmade” craft without café-rustic kitsch. Think walnut desk, warm paper, brass hardware — not barn wood or latte foam.
- **Colours:**
  - Walnut `#5C4033` (primary brand / wordmark weight)
  - Espresso `#2C2118` (near-black text / dark sections)
  - Warm ivory `#F3EDE4` (page ground)
  - Parchment `#E8DFD2` (secondary surfaces)
  - Brass `#C4A574` (accent — CTAs, fine lines, focus)
  - Soft charcoal `#3D3833` (body text)
- **Fonts:** Fraunces (display / “CarterMade”) + DM Sans (UI / body)
- **Tone:** Calm confidence. Craftsman who ships modern sites. Never loud, never corporate-agency.
- **Logo:** Wordmark-first — “CarterMade” as the hero signal. Logo asset TBD.

## Notes

- **Not a local-business homepage pattern** — this is a studio portfolio. No hero map, no NAP-first GEO layout.
- Animations: subtle, scroll-timed, puzzle-piece reveals — never bounce, floaters, or hero stickers.
- First viewport: brand + one headline + one line + CTA group + one dominant visual only.
- WhatsApp is the primary lead path; keep friction near zero.
- Avoid purple gradients, cream+terracotta cliché, broadsheet newspaper layouts.
